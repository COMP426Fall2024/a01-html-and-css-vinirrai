Famous Temples Dedicated to Lord Hanuman

Lord Hanuman is worshipped in many temples across India, each known for its unique history and religious significance. Here are some of the most famous temples dedicated to Lord Hanuman:

Temple Name Location
Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh
Jakhu Temple Shimla, Himachal Pradesh
Hanuman Garhi Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh
Namakkal Anjaneyar Temple Namakkal, Tamil Nadu

Each of these temples is an important pilgrimage site for devotees of Lord Hanuman, and visiting these temples is believed to bring blessings and protection from harm.
