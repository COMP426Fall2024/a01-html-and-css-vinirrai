The Legend of Lord Hanuman

Lord Hanuman, known as the son of Anjana and Kesari, is one of the most revered figures in Hindu mythology. His loyalty, devotion, and strength have made him a hero in many legends, most notably the Ramayana. According to the epic, Hanuman played a crucial role in helping Lord Rama rescue Sita from the clutches of Ravana, the demon king of Lanka.
