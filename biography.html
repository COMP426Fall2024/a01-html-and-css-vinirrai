Biography of Lord Hanuman

Lord Hanuman, also known as Anjaneya, is revered as a symbol of strength, devotion, and loyalty. Born to Anjana and Kesari, Hanuman is believed to be an incarnation of Lord Shiva. His loyalty to Lord Rama and his significant role in the Ramayana made him one of the most revered deities in Hinduism.

From his childhood pranks to his monumental feats of courage in assisting Lord Rama, the life and stories of Hanuman are filled with inspiration and devotion, serving as a symbol of dedication and faith for devotees around the world.
