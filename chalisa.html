Hanuman Chalisa

The Hanuman Chalisa is a 40-verse devotional hymn composed by the saint-poet Tulsidas. It is one of the most revered prayers recited by millions of devotees across the world. The Chalisa praises the strength, courage, and devotion of Lord Hanuman and is believed to grant protection and blessings to those who chant it with faith.

Shree Guru Charan Saroj Raj, Nij manu Mukuru Sudhaari
Barnau Raghuvar Bimal Jasu, Jo daayak Phal Chaari
Budhiheen Tanu Jannikay, Sumirow Pavan Kumar
Bal Buddhi Vidya Dehu Mohi, Harahu Kalesh Vikar
