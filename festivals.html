Festivals Celebrating Hanuman

Lord Hanuman is celebrated in various festivals throughout the year. These festivals are particularly important in the northern parts of India and in temples dedicated to Hanuman around the world. The most prominent festival is:

  • Hanuman Jayanti - Celebrated in April, Hanuman Jayanti marks the birth of Lord Hanuman. Devotees perform special prayers, chant the Hanuman Chalisa, and offer sweets and garlands at temples.
  • Tuesday Offerings - Many devotees offer special prayers to Hanuman every Tuesday, which is considered an auspicious day to worship him.
  • Special Pujas during Ram Navami - Hanuman is also celebrated during Ram Navami, as his loyalty and service to Lord Rama are central to the Ramayana.
